The buyer journey

Why health-niche marketing is completely different
from what you've been doing.

When a potential client lands on your page, they don't immediately want to reach out to you, even if they need the transformation you're offering. They go through invisible stages and if your content isn't designed to meet them at each stage, they'll leave without ever telling you why.

Stage 1
RecognitionThey stop scrolling because something you said feels uncomfortably familiar. "This person is talking about something I'm experiencing."
Stage 2
IdentificationThey go through your page, looking for proof that you get the full picture, not just the surface symptoms. "She actually understands this. Like, really understands."
Stage 3
BeliefThey need to believe your solution is designed for someone exactly like them. "She knows what's wrong AND she has a solution."
Stage 4
TrustHealth clients don't buy from strangers. This stage takes time and every post you make either builds it or erodes it. "I think she's the one I should work with."
Stage 5
ActionThis is where they DM you, book the call, click the link. But they only get here if you've walked them through all four stages before this. "I need to reach out. I don't want to feel like this anymore."

Generic marketing skips straight to "here's my offer, book a call." Health marketing builds the journey. The difference is everything.
